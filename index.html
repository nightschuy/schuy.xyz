About

Schuyler Hample
(SKY-ler)

Charleston, SC Fort Lauderdale, FL Miami, FL

Schuyler Hample, aka Schuy, is a digital artist and designer operating between Charleston and South Florida — a practice built at the intersection of visual art, motion, and high-end brand design.

The work lives in the space between the cinematic and the synthetic: deep color, geometric tension, and an obsession with light that feels both engineered and alive. Whether it's a fine art print, a stage visual, or a design system, the goal is always the same — immersive digital imagery at the highest possible pixel count.

Available for select collaborations, commissions, and creative direction.
